Our Mission

A business with a conscience.

Our principal motto is to develop and commercialize technologies for producing cost-competitive bio-renewable fuels, chemicals, and materials — enabling communities and regions around the world to effectively and sustainably meet their needs.

01
Renewable

Be renewable.

We manufacture renewable fuels, chemicals, and materials — including cellulosic ethanol. Our outputs serve industrial, pharmaceutical, nutraceutical, and agricultural applications, all derived from biomass.

02
Global · Local

Be global.
Be local.

Technologies designed for communities worldwide — enabling every region to meet its energy and material needs sustainably, cost-competitively, and with locally available feedstocks.

03
Environmental

Be environmental.

We tap into nature's resources to power our future. As society evolved, our energy needs came full circle — back to biofuels as a sustainable source of energy with dramatically reduced carbon impact.

04
Sustainable

Be sustainable.

A platform cannot be sustainable long-term if it isn't competitive as a business — and today's businesses cannot be competitive if they aren't attentive to sustainability. Both are prerequisites.

Why biomass

From agricultural residue to high-value renewable products.

Ligno-cellulosic biomass is nature's most abundant raw material. We turn it into commercial-grade fuels and chemicals — without competing with food supply.

  • Non-food feedstocks. Agricultural residues, energy crops, and forestry byproducts.
  • Dual-sugar recovery. Both C5 (pentose) and C6 (hexose) streams commercialized.
  • Economic viability. Best-in-class process economics versus petrochemical alternatives.
  • Carbon reduction. Dramatically lower emissions versus fossil-derived counterparts.

The Platform

Patented biomass conversion scaled a million-fold.

For nearly two decades, SuGanit has developed and patented a robust platform to produce five-carbon (C5) and six-carbon (C6) sugars and their derivatives from ligno-cellulosic biomass — with critical attention to process scalability and economic viability.

01
Biomass Sourcing

Agricultural residues, energy crops, forestry byproducts — locally available, globally consistent.

02
Pre-treatment

Proprietary process breaks down lignocellulose structure, maximizing sugar accessibility.

03
Sugar Extraction

Selective C5 & C6 streams isolated with high purity via patented separation technology.

04
Conversion

Biochemical routes to ethanol, specialty chemicals, and platform molecules.

05
End Products

Fuels, pharma inputs, nutraceuticals, agricultural chemicals — one unified platform.

About Us

Science, engineering, and vision — combined.

While globalization and free trade are good practices, societies and communities enjoy greater and more sustainable freedoms when they are locally self-sufficient in their necessities.
— SuGanit founding principle

SuGanit Bio-Renewables is a US-based organization with deep skills in science, engineering, and finance, coupled with an intrinsic vision of sustainable development. Our team includes several members with PhD degrees, MBAs, and graduate degrees in science and engineering.

Founded by Praveen Paripati, SuGanit was created to actively participate in transitioning the world from fossil-fuel dependence for transportation, chemicals, and materials to a more sustainable framework. Since inception, we have focused on both technology development and economic viability — a twin focus that produces platforms that are best-in-class, both environmentally and commercially.
