Technology Platform

Patented biomass
conversion at scale

Nearly two decades of R&D have produced a robust, patented platform to extract C5 & C6 sugars and derivatives from ligno-cellulosic biomass — scaled a million-fold with proven process economics.

Step 01
Biomass Sourcing

Agricultural residues, energy crops, and forestry byproducts — locally available, globally consistent.

Step 02
Pre-treatment

Proprietary pre-treatment breaks down lignocellulose structure, maximizing sugar accessibility.

Step 03
Sugar Extraction

Selective C5 & C6 sugar streams isolated with high purity via patented separation technology.

Step 04
Conversion

Biochemical conversion routes to ethanol, specialty chemicals, and platform molecules.

Step 05
End Products

Fuels, pharma inputs, nutraceuticals, agricultural chemicals — from one unified platform.

About Us

Science, engineering,
and vision — combined.

"While globalization and free trade are good practices, societies enjoy greater and more sustainable freedoms when they are locally self-sufficient in their necessities."

SuGanit Bio-Renewables is a US-based organization with deep expertise in science, engineering, and finance, coupled with an intrinsic vision of sustainable development. Our team includes members with PhD degrees, MBAs, and graduate degrees across science and engineering disciplines.

Since inception, SuGanit has focused its attention on both technology development and economic viability. This twin focus, coupled with out-of-the-box thinking and proprietary product development, has enabled us to successfully collaborate and develop patented technology that makes our production goals viable and best-in-class.
